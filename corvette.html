OVERVIEW The 2022-23 Chevrolet Corvette Z06 is a high-performance sports car that is built for speed and agility. The Z06 also features a lightweight carbon fiber body, advanced aerodynamics, and a unique suspension system that provides exceptional handling and grip.
Inside, the Z06 has a sporty and luxurious cabin, with leather and suede upholstery, a 12-inch digital instrument cluster, and an optional Bose Performance Series sound system. It also comes with advanced safety features, such as forward collision alert, lane departure warning, and rear cross-traffic alert.
Overall, the 2022-23 Chevrolet Corvette Z06 is a high-performance sports car that offers exceptional speed, handling, and technology. Its powerful engine, advanced aerodynamics, and luxurious features make it a top choice for car enthusiasts who want a sports car that delivers a thrilling driving experience.
